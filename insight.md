# Business Insights

## Sales by Category
Technology generated the highest sales, which indicates it is the strongest revenue-generating product category.

## 2. Sales by Region
The West region recorded the highest sales, while the South generated the lowest.

## 3. Sales by Trend
Sales increased during the second half of the year, showing stronger performance in later months.

## 4. Sales by Segment
The Consumer segment contributed the largest share of total sales.
